Optyca works with organisations facing complex planning, scheduling, and resource allocation challenges, particularly where manual or spreadsheet-based processes are no longer sufficient.

How We Work

  2. Build the Model – We translate real-world rules into mathematical optimisation models.
  3. Optimise & Test – We generate and test solutions to ensure feasibility and impact.
  4. Deliver Practical Tools – We provide clear outputs, dashboards, or decision-support tools.
